About Us

Welcome to HowAboutThisWeather.com! We bring you the weather for the towns of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven, Idaho. Along with current weather conditions and a 5-day forecast, you will also find local news articles. If you have a storm to report, be sure to visit our storm center. Finally, check out the gallery to see featured work of Idaho photographers. Thanks for stopping by!
