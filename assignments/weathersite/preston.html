Five Injured in Crash Near Preston

By Necia P. Seamons

Five people were injured in an accident at about 1 p.m. on Wednesday in Fairview, which is near Preston, police said. A 2013 Hyundai Elantra driven by Marshall T. Winder, of Preston, collided with a 2007 Toyota Corolla driven by Jenefer Arreola, also of Preston, at 3200 South and State Street, police said. According to the police report, the Hyundai was heading eastbound on 3200 South and Areola was heading southbound on State Street. The Hyundai failed to stop at the stop sign at the intersection and collided with the passenger side of the Toyota, police said.
