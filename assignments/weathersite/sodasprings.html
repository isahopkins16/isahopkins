Flood Watch Issued for East Idaho through Sunday

By National Weather Service

The National Weather Service has issued a flood watch throughout East Idaho based on the current forecast. The flood watch will begin Friday and continue through Sunday afternoon. Mild temperatures combined with several rounds of rain showers will cause continued snow melt and runoff. If you observe flooding, please report your observations to the National Weather Service in Pocatello via phone, e-mail, Facebook, or Twitter, when you can do so safely.
