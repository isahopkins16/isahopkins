Welcome!

Travelling long distance to attend the temple? Serving as a full-time temple missionary? We invite you to come stay at the Temple Inn & Suites. Whether you're here for one night or long-term, travelling by yourself or with the whole family, we have a room or a suite for you. We know temple attendance is a high priority, so we want to make it as convenient as possible!

Our First Location: St. George, Utah

Built in 1871 and located in sunny southern Utah, less than 4 blocks away from the St. George temple is where you can find our original location. This was the first temple completed in Utah. While you're here, be sure to check out the visitors' center.
